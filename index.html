About

Elan Hugo brings careful, experienced hands to every project — from delicate paste-the-wall installations to bold mural papers, custom paint finishes, and refined trim carpentry. Based in Vermont's Northern Champlain Valley, serving homes and businesses with the attention they deserve.

What we do

Craftsmanship across every surface

01

Wallpaper Installation

Precision hanging of paste-the-wall, traditional prepaste, and specialty papers — grasscloth, murals, seamed patterns, and more. Prep, sizing, and clean finishing included.

02

Wallpaper Removal

Safe, thorough removal of old wallcoverings with full wall prep and skim coating to leave surfaces smooth and ready for whatever comes next.

03

Painting

Interior and exterior painting with meticulous prep. Clean lines, durable finishes, and honest color consultation to make your space feel exactly right.

04

Finish Carpentry

Baseboards, crown molding, casing, wainscoting, built-ins, and custom trim work. The details that make a room feel complete.
