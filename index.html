Wallpaper installation & removal and finish carpentry, serving the Northern Champlain Valley.

Elan Hugo is a wallpaper contractor based in North Ferrisburgh — bringing careful hands and honest work to homes across northern Vermont.

Services
  • Wallpaper Installation

    Paste-the-wall, pre-pasted, and traditionally hung papers. Grasscloth, murals, large repeats, seamed patterns, tricky corners — handled with patience and precision. Proper prep and sizing included.

  • Wallpaper Removal

    Full removal of old wallcoverings, adhesive, and backing. Walls are skim-coated and sanded smooth before anything new goes up.

  • Finish Carpentry

    Baseboards, crown molding, door and window casing, chair rail, wainscoting, and custom trim. The details that make a room feel finished.

  • Surface Prep & Painting

    Brush and roller work where it supports a wallpaper or carpentry project — accent walls, adjacent trim, ceilings.
